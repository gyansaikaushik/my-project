Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional for spice)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, paprika, oregano, salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  2. Rub the mixture evenly over both sides of the chicken breasts.
  3. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for better flavor).
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes per side or until fully cooked (internal temp: 165°F / 75°C).
  6. Remove from grill and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite side dish. Enjoy!
