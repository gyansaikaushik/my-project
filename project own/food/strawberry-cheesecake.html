Strawberry Cheesecake üçì

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup strawberry jam

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325¬∞F (163¬∞C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 2 tbsp sugar. Press into the bottom of the pan.
  3. In a bowl, beat cream cheese and 1 cup sugar until smooth.
  4. Add vanilla and eggs one at a time, beating after each addition.
  5. Stir in sour cream and pour mixture over the crust.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes or until the center is set. Let cool completely.
  7. Spread strawberry jam on top and arrange fresh strawberries.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
