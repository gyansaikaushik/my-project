Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and cook the pancetta/bacon until crispy. Add minced garlic and saut√© briefly.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, then add the drained spaghetti to the pan and toss well.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring continuously to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Add a little reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately. Enjoy!
