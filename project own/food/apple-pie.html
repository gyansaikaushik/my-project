Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup ice water
  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Prepare pie crust by mixing flour, butter, and salt. Add ice water and form a dough.
  3. Roll out half the dough and place in a pie pan.
  4. Mix apples with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice, then add to the crust.
  5. Roll out the second crust and place it over the filling.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Cool before serving.
