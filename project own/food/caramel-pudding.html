Caramel Pudding üçÆ

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Prepare a water bath by placing a baking dish in a larger pan filled with hot water.
  2. In a saucepan, heat sugar and water over medium heat until golden caramel forms.
  3. Quickly pour caramel into the baking dish, tilting to coat the bottom evenly.
  4. In a bowl, whisk eggs, condensed milk, whole milk, vanilla, and salt until smooth.
  5. Pour the mixture over the caramel and place the dish in the water bath.
  6. Bake for 45-50 minutes until set. Cool and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  7. Run a knife around the edges and invert onto a plate to serve.
