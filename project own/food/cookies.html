Chocolate Chip Cookies üç™

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar together until fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto the baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Cool on a wire rack and enjoy!
